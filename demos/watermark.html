Watermark.sampo-watermark

Big faint mill for section backgrounds. 0.08 opacity in light mode, 0.12 in dark. Pair with a positioned parent. Slow scroll-linked rotation arrives in Phase 5.

A section with a watermark

The watermark sits on z-index: 0 and position: absolute; content reads over it via a positioned stacking context. 

<section style="position: relative; overflow: hidden;">
  <div class="sampo-watermark" aria-hidden="true"></div>
  <div style="position: relative; z-index: 1;">…content…</div>
</section>
